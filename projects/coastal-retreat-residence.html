Coastal Retreat Residence

Client

The Carter Family

COMPLETION

October 7, 2024

PROJECT TYPE

Interior Transformation

DESIGNER

Daniel White

A tranquil coastal sanctuary where nature, light, and design blend seamlessly. Coastal Retreat Residence is a carefully crafted interior transformation that embraces the relaxed charm of beachfront living while integrating refined contemporary elements.

An Open and Airy Layout

The residence was reimagined to create an effortless connection between the indoors and the surrounding coastal landscape. Walls were removed to open up the living areas, allowing ocean breezes and natural light to flow through the space. Sliding glass doors lead to an expansive terrace, blurring the boundary between interior and exterior. The master bedroom, positioned for optimal views, features private access to a sun-drenched balcony, perfect for morning coffee with a seaside backdrop.

Natural Materials for a Relaxed Aesthetic

The material palette reflects the textures of the coast, incorporating light oak flooring, whitewashed wooden beams, and soft linen drapery. A handcrafted stone fireplace serves as the heart of the living area, adding a rustic yet elegant touch. The kitchen is outfitted with custom white cabinetry, marble countertops, and rattan barstools, reinforcing the home's breezy, natural aesthetic.

A Light-Filled and Harmonious Atmosphere

Lighting design played a key role in enhancing the residence’s calm and welcoming environment. Oversized rattan pendant lights hang above the dining table, casting a warm and diffused glow. In the bedrooms, delicate linen-covered bedside lamps provide soft illumination, while recessed lighting in the hallways subtly highlights architectural elements without overpowering the space. Skylights were introduced to amplify daylight in key areas, further brightening the home.

Bespoke Furnishings Inspired by Coastal Living

Custom furniture was carefully selected to complement the home's natural theme while offering comfort and functionality. The living room features a deep-seated sectional in off-white linen, accompanied by a reclaimed wood coffee table that adds a touch of organic character. Bedrooms are adorned with woven headboards and soft, layered textiles that invite relaxation. Throughout the home, handcrafted ceramics and artisanal decor pieces reflect the authenticity of coastal craftsmanship.

A True Coastal Escape

Coastal Retreat Residence is designed as a serene getaway where every element evokes a sense of ease and connection to nature. Through the use of organic materials, open spaces, and an effortless flow between indoors and outdoors, the design captures the essence of relaxed coastal living while maintaining a refined, timeless elegance. Whether enjoying the panoramic ocean views or unwinding in the tranquil interiors, this home is a true retreat from the everyday.
