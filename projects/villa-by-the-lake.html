Villa by the Lake

Client

Davenport Family

COMPLETION

October 12, 2023

PROJECT TYPE

Private Residence

DESIGNER

Jonathan Wells

A lakeside sanctuary where architecture and nature exist in perfect harmony. Villa by the Lake was designed as an immersive retreat, offering panoramic views, seamless indoor-outdoor connections, and a deep respect for its natural setting. Every element of the home is crafted to enhance tranquility and provide a timeless living experience.

Framing the Perfect View

The villa’s placement was meticulously planned to capture the serene beauty of the lake while maintaining privacy from neighboring properties. Expansive glass façades open toward the water, allowing natural light to flood the interiors throughout the day. Thoughtfully positioned terraces and balconies extend living spaces outdoors, creating the perfect setting for relaxation or entertaining guests. The gentle slope of the site ensures that every room enjoys a direct visual connection to the water, reinforcing the sense of calm and openness.

Seamless Indoor-Outdoor Living

A defining feature of the villa is its effortless transition between indoor and outdoor spaces. Large sliding glass doors eliminate barriers, allowing residents to move freely between the comfort of the interior and the beauty of the lakeside landscape. An open-air courtyard at the heart of the home acts as a private oasis, while covered terraces provide year-round outdoor living opportunities, regardless of the season. The material palette—featuring warm wood, textured stone, and smooth concrete—creates a sense of cohesion between the built and natural environments.

A Timeless Interior with Natural Warmth

Inside, the villa embraces a refined yet cozy atmosphere. The open-plan living area is centered around a stone fireplace, creating a welcoming focal point. The kitchen, featuring handcrafted wooden cabinetry and state-of-the-art appliances, is designed for both functionality and aesthetic appeal. Soft, neutral tones dominate the interiors, allowing the surrounding landscape to become the true artwork of the home. Large windows frame ever-changing views of the lake, bringing a dynamic quality to every space.

Services Provided

Masterplan

A thoughtfully designed villa positioned to maximize lake views while ensuring privacy. The layout balances open communal areas with secluded retreats, creating a harmonious living experience.

Materials

A carefully curated selection of natural materials, including timber, stone, and glass, to enhance durability and blend with the lakeside environment. The result is a timeless aesthetic that complements its surroundings.

Energy

A self-sufficient energy concept incorporating solar panels, geothermal heating, and water recycling. Smart home technology optimizes energy use, reducing environmental impact without compromising comfort.
