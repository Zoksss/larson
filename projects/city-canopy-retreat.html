City Canopy Retreat

Client

The Harrison Family

COMPLETION

December 11, 2024

PROJECT TYPE

Private Lodge

DESIGNER

Benjamin Scott

A modern retreat in the heart of the city, designed for both privacy and elegance. Urban Haven Residence redefines city living by integrating sleek architecture with warm natural elements, creating a sophisticated yet welcoming urban home.

A Contemporary Facade That Stands Out

The exterior of the residence combines clean, geometric forms with rich textural contrasts. A striking black metal frame outlines the facade, complemented by floor-to-ceiling glass panels that flood the interiors with natural light. Natural stone cladding adds an element of warmth and durability, while wooden slats provide both aesthetic appeal and functional privacy for the upper-level balconies. The main entrance is emphasized by a recessed wooden portal, creating a sense of depth and grandeur.

Landscaping That Merges Nature and Urbanity

Despite its central location, the house incorporates lush greenery to soften the urban surroundings. Vertical gardens climb the side walls, bringing life to the exterior, while a private courtyard provides a tranquil escape from the city's hustle and bustle. A rooftop garden with a wooden deck and integrated seating offers a secluded outdoor retreat, complete with panoramic city views. Carefully placed planters along the facade introduce natural elements without compromising space.

Strategic Exterior Lighting for Aesthetic and Security

The lighting design plays a crucial role in defining the home’s nighttime character. Recessed LED strips highlight the architectural lines, creating a soft yet striking glow. Pathway lighting guides residents and guests toward the entrance, while wall-mounted fixtures add subtle illumination to outdoor seating areas. Smart lighting controls allow adjustments based on time of day and activity, ensuring both ambiance and security.

Blurring the Boundaries Between Indoors and Outdoors

Large sliding glass doors connect the main living area to the private courtyard, seamlessly blending indoor and outdoor spaces. A partially covered terrace extends the living space, offering an ideal area for entertaining. The rooftop garden doubles as an outdoor lounge, complete with a firepit and minimalist furniture, providing a serene retreat above the bustling streets.

An Icon of Urban Elegance

Urban Haven Residence proves that city homes can be both luxurious and intimately connected to nature. With a carefully curated facade, functional green spaces, and innovative lighting, the design embraces modern urban aesthetics while maintaining a warm and inviting presence. This residence stands as a testament to refined city living, offering both privacy and vibrancy in equal measure.
